ROBOTS 64

The game concept is somehow similar to Gnome Robots (https://wiki.gnome.org/Apps/Robots)

Goal:
Escape the robots and lure them into the mines (X).
Avoid robots, mines and walls.
You can temporarily freeze them for a limited number of steps by using a limited number of freezing bombs.
Every fifth killed robot you are awarded an extra freezing bomb.
If you do not kill any robot for too long, you die anyway.

Controls:
Move with I J K L
Activate freezing bomb: Z
